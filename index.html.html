Active & Passive Voice

Duas formas de dizer a mesma coisa — com foco diferente.

🔁 Transformação
Subject
+
verb
+
object
↓ Passiva = objeto vira sujeito
Object
+
to be + past participle
(+ by ...)
Active
Diogenes serves amazing drinks.
→
Passive
Amazing drinks are served by Diogenes.
Active
They built the rooftop last year.
→
Passive
The rooftop was built last year.
Active
We will open Meduza soon.
→
Passive
Meduza will be opened soon.
💡 Quando usar a passiva? Quando a ação é mais importante do que quem a faz.
"Meduza was inspired by Greek mythology." 🐍
Grammar Review

Present Perfect

Conecta o passado ao presente. Muito usado em inglês!

Fórmula: HAVE/HAS + Past Participle
I / You / We / They
+
have
+
particípio
He / She / It
+
has
+
particípio

Exemplos

I have visited Meduza.
He has opened a rooftop.
We have worked hard.
Have you ever been here?

Palavras-chave

already — já
yet — ainda
ever — alguma vez
never — nunca
just — acabou de
since / for
🏛️ Exemplos do Meduza
Diogenes has worked on this for months.
Diogenes trabalhou nisso por meses.
We have already designed the menu.
Já projetamos o cardápio.
Have you ever been to a rooftop bar?
Você já foi a um bar de rooftop?
I have never seen a view like this.
Nunca vi uma vista assim.
⚡ Perfect vs Past:
"I went to Meduza." — tempo específico (ontem)
"I have been to Meduza." — sem tempo específico
